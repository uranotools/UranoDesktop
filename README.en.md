Welcome to the new era of productivity with **Urano Desktop**. This application is not just a chat; it is a complete ecosystem designed to give you total control over intelligent, modular AI agents that are, above all, capable of acting on your real environment.

Urano is both a **personal productivity tool** ready to use, and an **advanced AI laboratory** with its own UI, orchestration system, and plugin community.

---

## 📥 Get Started in 3 Steps

Installing and starting to work with your own assistants is incredibly simple:

1. **Download and Install**: Available natively for **Windows and macOS**. Visit [uranoai.com](https://uranoai.com).
2. **Connect your favorite AI**: Native support for **12 AI providers** (OpenAI, Anthropic, Google, DeepSeek, xAI, local Ollama, Alibaba, Moonshot, and more). Just enter your API Key and you're ready!
3. **Create your Agent**: Assign a personality, activate the integrations you need, and start delegating tasks.

---

## 🌟 Native Features and Capabilities

Urano lives on your computer, respecting your privacy and allowing the AI to interact deeply with your desktop environment:

- 👁️ **Vision and Control (SystemEye)**: Your agents can "see" your screen through captures, and control your mouse and keyboard in a supervised manner.
- 💬 **Floating Bubble (MiniChat)**: Keep your assistant always at hand over any window on your computer.
- ⚡ **Native OS Notifications**: Agents work in the background and send you desktop push notifications when they finish a report or task. Click the notification to go directly back to the chat!
- 🔄 **Multiverse of Agents (MultiTabs)**: Multiple parallel chats. A programming agent in one tab can delegate web searches to a research agent in another tab simultaneously.
- 📊 **Interactive Dynamic UI**: Agents can generate mini-applications in real-time (dashboards, charts, control panels) instead of just outputting text.

## 🔌 Plugin Ecosystem: Urano Marketplace

Extend your agents' capabilities with a single click!

Urano includes a **decentralized Marketplace** of MCP (Model Context Protocol) modules. Through it, you can install connectors so your agents can operate with:
- **Google Workspace** (Docs, Sheets, Drive, Calendar, Gmail)
- **Development** (GitHub, Local Terminal, File Search)
- **Research** (Brave Search, Tavily, Serper)
- **Productivity** (Slack, Jira, Notion)

**1-Click Updates**: The Urano system constantly checks the global community registry and notifies you if there are updates available for your installed plugins. Everything is managed in an encrypted local vault (Vault) to keep your passwords and tokens secure on your machine.
